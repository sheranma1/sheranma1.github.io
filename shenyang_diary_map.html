Interactive Route Map: Shenyang Diary

The Journey from Chosŏn to Shenyang · 1637

The Shenyang Diary (瀋陽日記) records the journey and subsequent hostage period of Crown Prince Sohyeon and his retinue following the Qing invasion of Chosŏn in 1636–37. This map illustrates 33 stops mentioned in the diary, tracking their route from Hanseong (Seoul) across the Yalu River to the Qing capital of Mukden (Shenyang).
