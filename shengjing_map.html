盛京輿地全圖

Shengjing Yudi Quantu — General Map of Shengjing Territories

盛京輿地全圖 (General Map of Shengjing) is a detailed cartographic representation of the territories surrounding Shengjing (modern Shenyang, Liaoning). This 1734 manuscript map illustrates the administrative divisions, geographical features, and military fortifications established during the Qing Dynasty in the Northeast.
